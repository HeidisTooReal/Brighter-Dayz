## Full description
Brighter Dayz is a warm, faith-filled safe space made for school-age children
(ages 5–14) to understand their feelings, find calm, and remember just how
loved they are.

Guided by Sunny the Lamb — a kind, encouraging buddy — children can:

🌈 Check in with their feelings
Tap a friendly face to share how they feel today, from "struggling" to "joyful,"
and receive a gentle, caring response.

💬 Talk to Sunny
A safe, kid-friendly buddy who listens, encourages, and gently points little
hearts toward hope, kindness, and God's love.

📖 Story Library
A growing shelf of read-aloud storybooks — beloved Bible stories, courageous
Bible heroes, and everyday situations kids can relate to (first days, sharing,
big feelings, friendship, and more), each ending with a gentle lesson.

🌬️ Breathe & Calm
Simple, age-appropriate breathing exercises with soothing guidance to help
worried hearts settle.

🙏 Faith for little hearts
A Prayer Corner and God's Promises (Scripture made simple) to nurture peace,
gratitude, and hope.

⭐ Encouragement & badges
Happy words, daily encouragement, and Sunshine Hero badges to celebrate every
child's journey.

👨‍👩‍👧 Built for families
Parents set up and supervise everything from a PIN-protected dashboard, can see
their child's check-ins, and receive caring safety alerts if a child shares
something concerning.

Brighter Dayz is designed to be gentle, ad-free, and full of kindness — a little
sunshine for your child's heart, every day.
